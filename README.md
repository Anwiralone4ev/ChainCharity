# ChainCharity

## Description
ChainCharity is a decentralized fundraising platform built on the Stellar network using Soroban Smart Contracts. The project addresses the critical lack of transparency in traditional charitable organizations. By leveraging blockchain technology, ChainCharity ensures that every donation is tracked, and funds can only be disbursed through a 2/3 multi-signature consensus among board members, eliminating the risk of individual mismanagement or fraud.

## Project Vision
ChainCharity aims to redefine global philanthropy by replacing blind faith with cryptographic proof. Our vision is to build a world where every donor—regardless of the amount contributed—has absolute certainty that their funds reach the intended cause. By eliminating intermediaries and implementing rigid, code-based governance (Multi-Sig), we strive to restore public trust in charitable institutions and ensure that aid reaches those in need with maximum efficiency and zero leakage.

## Features
* **Multi-Sig Governance (2/3):** Funds are protected by a multi-signature wallet. Proposals for fund allocation require at least 2 out of 3 admins to approve before the transaction is executed.
* **Transparent Donation Tracking:** Donors can verify the total funds available and track their individual contribution history directly on the ledger.
* **Proposal-Based Allocation:** Every expenditure must be documented with a clear description (e.g., IPFS hashes for invoices or project details) and created as a formal proposal.
* **Automated Execution:** Once the approval threshold is met, the Smart Contract automatically transfers the funds to the beneficiary, removing the need for intermediaries.
* **Real-time Auditing:** All events (Donate, Create Proposal, Approve, Execute) are published as blockchain events for easy auditing.
